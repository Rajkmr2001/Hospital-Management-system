About Our Hospital

Leading the way in medical excellence, innovation, and patient care since 1975.

Our Story

Founded in 1975, our hospital has been at the forefront of medical innovation and patient care for over 45 years. What started as a small community clinic has grown into one of the region's most respected healthcare institutions.

Our commitment to excellence in healthcare delivery, research, and medical education has earned us numerous accolades and the trust of countless patients and their families.
