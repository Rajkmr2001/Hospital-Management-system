Healthy Living Tips

Small changes can lead to big results. Start with these evidence-based recommendations.

Balanced Nutrition

Focus on whole foods: vegetables, fruits, lean proteins, and whole grains. Practice portion control and mindful eating.

Hydration

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Often thirst is mistaken for hunger. Limit sugary beverages.

Sleep Quality

Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep nightly. Poor sleep disrupts hunger hormones and metabolism.
